Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies. Almost all websites use cookie technology. Cookies are files that are downloaded to the user's computer when accessing a website or some parts of a website. Among other things, cookies allow us to store and update information about a user's or device's browsing preferences. They also provide security when you visit our website and ensure that the website looks consistent and matches your preferences.

Types of cookies

Depending on who manages the domain, where the cookies are sent from and who processes the data received, there are two types of cookies: own cookies and third-party cookies.

There is also a second classification based on the time they are stored in the browser on the client computer: a distinction is made between session cookies and persistent cookies.

Finally, there is another classification that includes five types of cookies according to the purpose of processing the data: technical cookies, personalization cookies, analytics cookies, advertising cookies and behavioral advertising cookies.

Types of cookies used on this site

Technical cookies: These are files that allow the user to browse the Internet through a web page, platform or application and access various options or services available on that page. So, for example, technical cookies allow you to control traffic and data transfer, monitor the status of the user's access session, allow the user to enter parts of the site with restricted access, remember, move items of the order in an online store. during the order purchase process, apply for registration or participation in an event, use security features while browsing the web, store content to stream video or audio files, or share content via social networks.

Personalization cookies: Allows the user to access the service with some general settings predefined in the user's terminal, such as language, type of browser the service is accessed, regional configuration of such access.

Cookies used on this website and used depending on the purpose of processing the data:

Analytics cookies. This type of cookie allows the tracking and analysis of user behavior of the websites they are linked to. The information collected by this type of cookie is used to measure visits to websites, applications or platforms in order to improve website performance based on analysis of visitor usage data, and to create navigation profiles of users of such sites, applications and platforms. For this purpose, we use third-party cookies, in particular the Google Analytics service. See the Google Analytics Privacy Center for more information .

How to disable cookies

You can allow, block or delete cookies installed on your computer by adjusting your browser settings. Below, we provide you with the main browsers information on this topic.

Internet Explorer: Tools / Internet Options / Privacy / Settings .

See Microsoft Support or Browser Help for more information.

Firefox: Tools / Options / Privacy / History.

See Mozilla Support or Browser Help for more information .

Chrome: Configuration / Show advanced options / Privacy / Content options .

See Google Support or Browser Help for more information .

Safari: Settings / Security .
