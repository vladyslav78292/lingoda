Customer Reviews

Positive Reviews

Many customers praised Lingoda for its convenient and flexible scheduling options, which allowed them to fit language learning into their busy lives. The live online classes with native-speaking teachers received positive feedback, with learners appreciating the interactive and immersive learning experience. The structured curriculum and alignment with the CEFR were also commended for providing a systematic and comprehensive approach to language learning.

Effective Learning

Several reviews highlighted the effectiveness of Lingoda's courses in improving language skills. Learners reported progress in speaking, listening, reading, and writing, attributing their success to the interactive classes and personalized attention from teachers.

Engaging Platform

Customers often mentioned that the Lingoda platform was user- friendly and intuitive. The availability of learning materials and resources to supplement the classes was appreciated for providing additional practice and reinforcement.
